About

I'm Peter Heywood, research student in the VRGraphicS research group of the Department of Computer Science at the University of Sheffield.

My research is in GPU accelerated microsimulation of transport systems, working towards a Ph.D. under the supervision of Dr Paul Richmond and Dr Steve Maddock.

When I manage to find some free time I work on my own code projects and regurally go kayaking (and very occasionaly compete).

Demonstrating

As a PhD student in the department, I am able to demonstrate lab sessions for taught students.

I'm currently a demonstrator for two modules:

  • COM1008 Web and Internet Technology
  • COM3503/COM4503/COM650 3D Computer Graphics
